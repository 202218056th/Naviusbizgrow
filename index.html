Gujarat Government Loan Schemes for MSMEs & Startups

Explore comprehensive government loan schemes available in Gujarat for micro, small, and medium enterprises. Get subsidies, low interest rates, and easy access to funding.

Gujarat Industrial Investment Corporation (GIIC) MSME Loan

  • Loan Amount: Up to ₹50 crore
  • Interest Rate: 9.75% to 12.25%
  • Tenure: Up to 10 years
  • Collateral: Required
  • Eligibility: Minimum age 18 years

Pradhan Mantri MUDRA Yojana (PMMY)

  • Shishu: Up to ₹50,000
  • Kishore: ₹50,001 to ₹5 lakh
  • Tarun: ₹5 lakh to ₹10 lakh
  • Collateral: Not required
  • Available through: All major banks and NBFCs

Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)

  • Loan Amount: Up to ₹1 crore
  • Interest Rate: 11% to 12%
  • Tenure: 3-7 years
  • Subsidy: 15% to 35%
  • Education: VIII standard pass

Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for MSMEs (CGTMSE)

  • Loan Amount: Up to ₹5 crore
  • Collateral: Not required
  • Tenure: 5-10 years
  • Guarantee Fee: 0.37% to 1.35%
  • Age: Minimum 18 years

Aatmanirbhar Gujarat Scheme for MSMEs

  • Interest Subsidy: Available for manufacturing sector
  • Capital Investment Subsidy: Up to ₹35 lakhs
  • Additional Benefits: For young entrepreneurs and differently-abled
  • Tenure: 5-7 years
  • Focus: Manufacturing MSMEs

Startup Gujarat Scheme

  • Seed Funding: Up to ₹30 lakh
  • Focus Sectors: AI, agri-tech, fintech, health-tech
  • Additional Support: Incubation and mentorship
  • Target: Tech-based startups
  • Government: State Government of Gujarat
